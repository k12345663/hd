Metric Definitions

  • Recall (Sensitivity): Proportion of actual heart disease cases correctly identified
  • AUROC: Model's ability to distinguish between disease and non-disease cases
  • Accuracy: Overall correctness of predictions
  • Precision: Proportion of predicted disease cases that are actually positive
  • F1 Score: Harmonic mean of precision and recall
  • Brier Score: Calibration quality of probability predictions (lower is better)
